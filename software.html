Software

Recently, I have been playing with Claude Code & building a few pieces of software to make my life easier. I am freely hosting them here in case they help others.

CoeAdvisor

CoeAdvisor is basically just a compilation of all of the various major/minor checklists. It lets you input courses, save a student's course history in a way that can be read in & out of the program, and evaluates a student's current and proposed course list against their requirements. If it proves useful to people other than me, maybe more things will be added.

Launch CoeAdvisor

Grader

Grader is flexible software for reading in & grading multiple choice answer sheets in a FERPA compliant way.

Launch Grader

Evo

Evo is a set of statistical and evolutionary tutorials along with R training. It is student-facing and will likely warrant its own page down the line.
