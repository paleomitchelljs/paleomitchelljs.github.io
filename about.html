Jonathan S. Mitchell

I received my undergraduate in Geology with a paleontology concentration from Appalachian State University. I worked on Triassic microvertebrate bonebeds with Dr. Andrew Heckert.

I did an internship working on Late Jurassic and Early Cretaceous microvertebrate bone beds with Dr. Matthew Carrano at the Smithsonian.

Then, I completed a Ph.D. at The University of Chicago in the Committee on Evolutionary Biology. I worked primarily at the Field Museum.
